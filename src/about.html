Introducing Remisal Audio, a web based pre-processing waveform tool.

Remisal Audio displays the waveform of an audio as a time series, allowing both vertical and horizontal scaling and descaling. Reverb, denoise, equalize and adjust tempo, pitch frequencies and a 20 band-token graphic EQ, compressor and more.

Browser and device support

:: Feature List ::
  • Visualization of waveform
  • Visualization of frequency levels
  • Hard Limiter and distortion signaling
  • Time-series synced audio trimming
  • Inverting and Reversing Audio
  • Multi-format Exporting
  • Fade In/Out
  • Compressor
  • Normalization
  • Reverb
  • Delay
  • Light memory buffer for undo and error correction
  • Offline enabled software.

Free for use

Getting Started

To get started, drag and drop an audio file, or try the included sample.
Once the waveform is displayed, have fun!.

Recording Audio

To record audio, simply press the Recording button, or the [R] key.

ExportingAudio

In order to export in any format, click on 'File', then select your desired format, and follow the modal's instructions.
